Last-Mile Cold Chain System

Portable Solar-Powered Vaccine Storage for Remote Regions

Download Project PDF

Problem Statement

Vaccines lose potency outside the 2–8°C range. Remote rural areas lack reliable electricity, leading to high wastage. Our project focuses on an affordable, portable, solar-powered cold chain system to maintain safe storage conditions.

Objectives

  • Maintain temperature between 2–8°C consistently
  • Run entirely on solar power to remove dependency on the electric grid
  • Provide portability for transportation in remote areas
  • Ensure vaccine safety and reduce spoilage percentage

System Design Summary

The cold chain system uses a combination of:

  • Solar charging unit
  • Vacuum insulated storage chamber
  • Phase Change Materials (PCM) for thermal storage
  • Microcontroller system (ESP32) for temperature monitoring

Full diagrams and architecture are available inside the PDF report.

Advantages & Impact

  • Eco-friendly due to solar power
  • Reliable during power outages
  • Low running cost, suitable for villages
  • Improves vaccine quality in remote areas
